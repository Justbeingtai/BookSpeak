About BookSpeak

BookSpeak came to life on July 6th, 2023, as a forum and online library to discover, review, and talk about your favorite stories. With it's calm yet intellectual aesthetic, BookSpeak aims to create a website that is accessible to readers big and small, and to rekindle the wonder of reading to those it has been lost upon.
